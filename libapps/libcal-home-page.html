Group Work and Study Spaces

Group study rooms are simple, enclosed meeting spaces seating up to 10 in the Richardson and Loop libraries. Reservable by current students, staff and faculty.

Group work tables seat from 6 to 8 people and exist in open areas of the Richardson Library.  Powered by media:scape technology, the tables allow users plug in computers and other devices and share content on single or double screens. Reservable by current students, staff and faculty.

Media Studios at Richardson Library

The media production studios include seating for 1 to 2 people. All studios include MacPros with single or double screens conducive to video and sound editing.

Microphones, cameras, keyboards, analog media players and other hardware can be attached to workstations to serve different purposes such as sound or video recording, presentation practice, podcasting, digitization, and digital preservation. Reservable by current students, staff and faculty.

Specialized Labs/Rooms at Richardson Library

Maker Hub

The Maker Hub is a new, experimental and creative space intended to connect DePaul researchers, makers, and entrepreneurs from across campus and beyond with specialized equipment and a collaborative atmosphere for design and innovation. Reservable by current DePaul student organizations, faculty and staff.

Studio G

Room of 6 group work tables seating 5 each with attached digital panels and resident computers allowing a large group to break apart into smaller teams to work with digital content. Also includes podium computer at lectern, overhead projector, and screen for presenters.  Mac laptops to plug into tables available by request.  Maximum capacity is 30.  Reservable by current student organizations, faculty and staff.
